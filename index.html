Modular Trackside Drainage Systems

At Indovia Rail, we believe the future of rail infrastructure lies in smart engineering and sustainable design. Our Modular Trackside Drainage Systems redefine how India manages water across its rail networks — from high-speed corridors to metro lines and industrial tracks. Built using advanced polymer technology crafted from 100% recycled materials, our drainage systems deliver uncompromising performance while supporting India’s commitment to a greener, more resilient infrastructure.

  • Deploy up to 60% faster than traditional concrete systems — minimizing downtime and maximizing operational efficiency.
  • Engineered with enhanced flow capacity and proven flood protection, ensuring uninterrupted rail operations in even the most challenging weather conditions.
  • Every Indovia system is tested and certified to meet the highest standards of railway infrastructure compliance and performance
  • Achieve up to 614 kg CO₂e savings per cubic meter compared to concrete alternatives — a small change that makes a big environmental impact.
  • Modular Design: Flexible system adapts to various trackside configurations and drainage requirements
  • Proven Durability: Engineered for the demanding conditions of railway environments

Essential for trackside areas, cutting drainage, platform water management, and any railway surface where effective water management is critical for operational safety and infrastructure protection.

Since 1991

A Proud History Spanning Three Decades in the Rail Industry

Indovia began its journey in 1991 when approached by Reidel Omni to develop a faster, more environmentally friendly polyurethane-based system for bonding rubber panels. Our innovative cold cure bonding process reduced curing time to just 1.5 hours, revolutionising production efficiency.

After purchasing Omni's moulds in 1999, we established Indovia and continue our dedication to innovation and sustainability in rail technology, collaborating with worldwide rail infrastructure firms to develop bespoke crossing solutions.
