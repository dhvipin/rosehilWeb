Three Decades of Rail Innovation

From pioneering cold-cure technologies in India to emerging as a trusted global leader in sustainable rail crossing solutions, Indovia Rail continues to set new standards in innovation, reliability, and long-term performance for modern rail networks worldwide.

Our Story

1991

The Beginning

Indovia, a subsidiary of Rosehill Polymers Group, entered the rail industry when approached by American company Reidel Omni to develop a revolutionary polyurethane-based bonding system for rubber railway crossing surfaces.

1990s

Innovation Breakthrough

Our team at Indovia Rail has engineered a breakthrough cold-cure bonding process that delivers exceptional performance while redefining industry standards. This advanced system cuts curing time down to just 1.5 hours, offering a remarkable upgrade over traditional vulcanisation methods that once required long periods of high-temperature heating. Designed for the demanding conditions of India’s modern rail networks, this innovation ensures faster installation, improved reliability, and reduced downtime—reflecting Indovia Rail’s commitment to smarter, more sustainable infrastructure solutions.

1999

Independence & Growth

When Omni ceased railway panel production, Rosehill seized the opportunity by purchasing their moulds and beginning our own production of railway crossing panels, marking the true start of Indovia.

Today

Global Leadership

Now a trusted industry leader, we collaborate with rail infrastructure firms worldwide, delivering bespoke crossing solutions that meet specific project requirements across all continents.

What Sets Us Apart

Bespoke Manufacturing

Every product is tailored to meet specific client needs. Our manufacturing process follows comprehensive operating instructions with meticulous documentation management within robust production and quality frameworks.

Precision & Quality

Indovia Rail operates under the highest global standards, holding ISO 9001:2015 certification and undergoing rigorous annual NQA audits. Our state-of-the-art, computer-controlled production facilities in India are managed by expert engineers and technicians, using precision-built machinery dedicated to manufacturing high-quality railway crossing panels.

Complete Traceability

Every Indovia Rail panel is digitally monitored throughout production, with complete data logs, weight verification, and thorough visual inspections. Each unit is clearly marked with its crossing grade, rail type, sleeper type, fastener specification, and year of manufacture—ensuring quick and accurate identification at any installation site.

Environmental Leadership

Crafted from 100% recycled rubber tyres and strengthened with advanced polyurethane binders, Indovia Rail panels are produced using our revolutionary cold-cure process. They are completely safe for the environment, with no chemical leaching and zero harmful vapour emissions, ensuring long-term sustainability for India’s rail infrastructure.

Exclusive Design Rights

All components in our level crossing systems are designed by Indovia, including those supplied under other names by our global distributors, ensuring consistent quality and innovation.

Strategic Location

Every component in our level crossing systems is engineered by Indovia Rail, even those distributed globally under different partner names. This ensures uniform quality, consistent performance, and the innovation that defines Indovia’s commitment to reliable rail solutions.
